# Purpose

The Reclaim Method™ is the proprietary client methodology developed by Reclaim Golf Performance LLC.

Its purpose is to provide every client with a structured, repeatable, assessment-first process that improves movement, performance, confidence, and long-term enjoyment of the game.

The methodology serves as the operational framework for every coaching program delivered by Reclaim Golf.

---

# Philosophy

We do not coach a golf swing.

We coach the golfer who owns the swing.

Every golfer is unique.

Every body moves differently.

Every improvement plan should reflect that individuality.

---

# The Eight Phases

1. Discover
2. Understand
3. Assess
4. Analyze
5. Build
6. Coach
7. Measure
8. Reclaim
# Phase 1 — Discover

Objective:

Understand the golfer before beginning formal assessment.

Topics include:

- Why are you here today?
- What frustrates you most?
- What are your goals?
- What does success look like?
- Golf history
- Current playing schedule
- Practice habits
- Previous instruction
