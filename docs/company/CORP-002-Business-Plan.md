# Executive Summary

Reclaim Golf Performance LLC is an evidence-informed golf performance consulting company dedicated to helping golfers improve movement, confidence, performance, and long-term enjoyment of the game.

Unlike traditional golf instruction that often begins by changing the golf swing, Reclaim Golf begins with a structured discovery and assessment process. Every client is evaluated as an individual by examining movement quality, physical capacity, golf performance, health history, injury history, lifestyle factors, and personal goals before coaching recommendations are made.

The company is intentionally designed as a premium consulting business built upon documented methodology, objective measurement, continuous education, responsible use of technology, and long-term client relationships.

Initial operations will leverage strategic partnerships with existing golf facilities, indoor simulator locations, and fitness centers to minimize financial risk while refining The Reclaim Method™, building intellectual property, and establishing a scalable business model.

The long-term vision is to become a nationally respected provider of evidence-informed golf performance consulting while advancing the profession through education, documentation, research, and continuous improvement.

# Company Overview

Reclaim Golf Performance LLC is a premium golf performance consulting company dedicated to helping golfers improve movement, confidence, performance, and long-term enjoyment of the game.

The company was founded on a simple belief:

The best golf instruction begins by understanding the golfer before attempting to change the golf swing.

Rather than relying solely on traditional swing instruction, Reclaim Golf combines movement assessment, golf biomechanics, exercise science, performance technology, corrective exercise principles, and individualized coaching to identify the underlying factors affecting performance.

Every recommendation is built around the individual golfer—not a standardized swing model.

The company is designed to serve golfers who want more than golf lessons. Clients receive a structured performance plan focused on improving movement quality, physical capacity, golf performance, and long-term health.
# The Problem

Millions of golfers experience declining performance as they age.

Distance decreases.

Consistency becomes difficult.

Pain becomes more common.

Recovery takes longer.

Most golfers assume these changes are simply part of getting older.

As a result, they often seek traditional golf instruction focused primarily on swing mechanics.

While swing instruction is valuable, it may not address the underlying physical, movement, or lifestyle factors influencing performance.

Many golfers are never shown why their performance changed.

Instead, they are simply taught what to change.

Reclaim Golf believes lasting improvement begins with understanding the reasons behind performance limitations before recommending solutions.

# Ideal Client Profile

Reclaim Golf is designed primarily for golfers between the ages of 55 and 70 who want to continue enjoying the game at a high level despite the physical changes that naturally occur with age.

The ideal client is often a successful professional, business owner, executive, or retiree who previously played at a much higher level than they do today. They remember what it felt like to drive the ball farther, strike the ball consistently, and play without pain.

Over time, career demands, family responsibilities, reduced practice time, injuries, and declining mobility have changed their body more than their desire to play great golf.

Many have invested in lessons, equipment, and technology but continue to struggle because the underlying physical limitations affecting their golf performance have never been fully evaluated.

Their primary goals are to:

- Play golf without pain.
- Regain confidence.
- Improve consistency.
- Recover lost distance where possible.
- Move better on and off the golf course.
- Continue enjoying golf for decades to come.

Success is not defined solely by lowering a handicap.

Success is measured by helping golfers move better, feel better, perform better, and enjoy the game throughout their lives.

# Financial Strategy

Reclaim Golf Performance LLC is being built as a sustainable, premium consulting business focused on long-term profitability rather than rapid expansion or high client volume.

The company's financial strategy prioritizes disciplined growth, operational efficiency, and continuous reinvestment in the client experience. During the early stages of the business, available capital and revenue will be directed toward methodology development, professional education, technology, marketing, documentation, and systems that improve service quality and scalability.

To minimize financial risk, Reclaim Golf will initially operate through strategic partnerships with golf facilities, indoor simulator locations, driving ranges, and fitness centers rather than investing in dedicated real estate. This lean operating model reduces fixed overhead while allowing the company to remain flexible as client demand and service offerings evolve.

The founder will initially build Reclaim Golf while maintaining full-time employment, transitioning to full-time business ownership only after defined financial and operational milestones have been achieved. This phased approach reduces financial risk, supports long-term sustainability, and allows the company to refine its methodology, client experience, and operational systems before scaling.

Revenue growth will be driven by increasing the lifetime value of each client through comprehensive assessments, individualized coaching programs, periodic reassessments, and ongoing performance support. As the business matures, additional recurring revenue opportunities—including digital education, online coaching, membership programs, workshops, licensing opportunities, and strategic partnerships—will diversify income while expanding the company's reach beyond local consulting services.

Financial decisions will be guided by long-term sustainability rather than short-term revenue. Investments will be evaluated based on their ability to improve client outcomes, strengthen the Reclaim Golf methodology, enhance operational efficiency, and support the company's mission of becoming a nationally respected provider of evidence-informed golf performance consulting.

In addition to financial capital, Reclaim Golf intentionally invests in intellectual property, documented methodologies, technology, research, and operational systems. These strategic assets are expected to increase the long-term value of the company by creating a scalable, repeatable coaching model that extends beyond the founder's individual expertise.
